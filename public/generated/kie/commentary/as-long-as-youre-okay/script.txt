"As long as you're okay."
Chapter 07 of The Life Lessons I Hope You Learn.


This song came from a single question I couldn't stop asking myself: A parent's silent vigil — the love that asks for nothing, only safety.

I wasn't looking for a clean answer. I was looking for the right way to carry the question.

When I wrote this, I kept coming back to certain images. doorway silhouette, sleeping child, lamp, phone glow, 2am. Not because they're poetic — because they're honest. These are the images that carry weight without trying to.

The lyrics started as a fragment: "inspiration / Everybody hides something / Behind a smile, behind a face / Little storms nobody notices." Everything else grew from that place.

If this song reaches you at the right moment — I hope it gives you permission.
Permission to ask. To sit with not knowing. To feel the weight without rushing to put it down.

That's all any of this is for.
